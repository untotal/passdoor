Features

Collect data by chat

Whatsform transforms regular forms to a chat interface, which is more familiar & less intimidating to your users.

Dynamic presentation

When you share the form link within Whatsapp, it displays dynamic data related to which users have filled the form last, total responses etc,

Signalling

Once the user fills up the form, she is encouraged to text a custom message back to the group, which increases your response rates.

Comparison with Google Forms

Google Forms is the go-to tool for form creation. However, in the last couple years, the advent of messaging platforms like Whatsapp has meant that Small businesses and professionals are increasingly interacting with their users directly within the messaging environment, rather than on email.

Whatsform is Google Forms optimized for messaging clients that optimizes data collection within messaging, implements signalling techniques and features that increase response rates and lets power users leverage messaging.
