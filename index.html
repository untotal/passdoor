Features

Electronic Codes

The Smart Lock opens with a 6 digit code that you generate on your mobile app. Send this OTP to the broker or tenant viewing the apartment.

Single Use

The OTP generated is for single use. The same broker/tenant will not be able to re-use the key to enter the apartment.

Attribution

You know when a code has been used to view the apartment.

Multiple Keys

Using the Passdoor mobile app you can create multiple OTP codes for different parties.

Long Term Codes

Once your house has been rented, you can enable your tenant to open the lock using their phone and/or a long term key code.

Destroy Keys

When a tenant departs your property, you can block all their previous codes.

No Proximity

You need not be near the lock to manage it. Do it from your mobile phone, anywhere. The Lock does not need any connectivity.

Our Story

Our founders have been renting a couple of apartments in Mumbai for the past 8 years. Every time the apartment gets vacant, they list it up on 99acres.com and Magicbricks.com.

When a broker wants to show the apartment to his customer, he needs the keys. Chabhi kahan hai? Well, the keys are at home or the office. So, he sends his boy to collect the keys, shows the apartment to his customer and then returns the keys. You know how many times this process repeats before a party likes the apartment and the lease is signed.

Once the tenant moves in, what about the old lock? Now, call us suspicious, but with dozens of brokers and their boys having handled the keys, its not too comforting and we dont want an incident. So they change the cylinder and the keys.

Every couple of years they realized that this process repeats and there is endless logistics involved in showing apartments. There had to be a better solution in today's day and age! So they worked on a electronic lock that lets landlords create OTP codes remotely and share them with the broker so he can get into the apartment. These are single use codes. You can be in another city and create codes using the mobile app. The lock surely doesn't need any Internet or wifi.
