Features

Phone is the Key

Use the Passdoor Mobile app on your Phone to open your lock.

Electronic Codes

A user who does not have a mobile phone can still open the lock using a numeric key.

Attribution

You know when your house has been opened. Choose to get notifications.

Manage Users

Create access for multiple members in your family. Adults having phones can use the app. Children and elderly can separate codes.

No Connectivity needed

The Passdoor lock does not require any Internet or connectivity to operate.

Restrict Keys

Restrict entry of a specific user whenever you want.

Use physical keys

You can still use the physical key to open the Passdoor lock.

Our Story

It's 2019. You are walking around with a Smartphone in your pocket. Accessing the web and watching Netflix.

But when you come back home, you are still fiddling in your pockets to fish out the keys to open the door.

To us, somehow, this doesn't fit. There has to be a better way. Your phone should be able to open the door.

We looked at solutions in the market. They sell for Rs. 20,000-45,000. Well, no wonder none of us have bought a digital lock!

We worked on this for a year and have come up with Passdoor - a Secure, Mobile Locking system that belongs in 2019.

Now use your phone to open the door. Don't have your phone? Use a code to open. Manage kids, elderly parents and help.

Our locks are priced at Rs 11,999.
